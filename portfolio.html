Week 8: Construct a graph that either supports/ refutes an argument that is made at a Talking Economics event.

During the talk on Health x Economics, Ben Moll highlighted the mistake in thinking that government lockdown led to economic crisis. He highlighted that it was rather the case that the health crisis led to the economic crisis because even in countries who went down the ‘no-lockdown’ route, such as Sweden, individuals still adjusted their behaviour in such a way that was detrimental to GDP. If we compare the economic impact of the pandemic on Denmark and Sweden, Denmark didn’t see drastically different additional output loss.
